Steps to find Resistance, Zener Voltage, Zener Power, Resistance Power of Zener Diode

The Zener diode is also known as the breakdown diode that is heavily doped in the semiconductor devices. It is a silicon semiconductor with the p-n junction, which is specially designed to work in the reverse biased condition. The main objective of the Zener diode is that it works as a voltage regulator to maintain the constant voltage.
