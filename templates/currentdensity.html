Steps to Calculate Current Density

Current Density Formula-

Electric Current Density = Electric Current/Area of Conductor


J = I/A


where:
  • Electric Current(i) - Electric Current is the time rate of flow of charge through a cross sectional area. (Measured in Ampere)
  • Area of Conductor(A) - Area of conductor is the measure of area swept. (Measured in Square Meter)
  • J is the Current Density
